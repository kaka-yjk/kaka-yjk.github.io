**Abstract:**
Multimodal Sentiment Analysis (MSA) involves interpreting emotions or sentiments from multiple data sources, such as text, audio, and video. We propose AMCCL, which uses convolutional layers with varying scales to capture features at different levels of granularity across different modalities. It employs contrastive learning techniques to better align and distinguish between different emotional states across the multimodal inputs, improving the model's robustness and accuracy.
